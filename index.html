Chemical Industry Accidents


Searchable Database of Indian Chemical Industry Accidents

There have been various accidents in Chemical Industries in recent years in India. These accidents have claimed the lives of many people and had a great impact on the lives of residents living in nearby areas. This project is an attempt to make a searchable database of all such accidents, and categorize the accidents in terms of casualties, damage, type of Industry and Economic loss.
